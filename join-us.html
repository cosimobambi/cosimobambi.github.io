Chinese Undergraduate and Graduate Students


Applications from Chinese undergraduate and graduate students are welcome at any time. Undergraduate students can apply for one of the FDUROP projects.

Interested candidates can contact us via email (bambi-at-fudan.edu.cn)




Non-Chinese Graduate Students


Non-Chinese students can apply for the Master Program or the PhD Program. Applications are accepted in October and November for positions starting in September of the year after.

The Master Program is 3 years and the student must already have a Bachelor Degree when the position starts. The PhD Program is 3 years and the student must already have a Master Degree when the position starts. 

Students can get either a CSC Scholarship or a Shanghai Government Scholarship. Every scholarship covers: tuition fee, accommodation inside the campus, salary (3000 CNY/month for Master students and 3500 CNY/month for PhD students), and medical insurance.

Interested candidates can contact us via email (bambi-at-fudan.edu.cn)

 



Visiting Positions for Undergraduate and Graduate non-Chinese Students


Undergraduate, Master, and PhD non-Chinese students from 183 non-Chinese universities* can apply for a China Link Scholarship to spend 1-12 months at Fudan University. Every scholarship covers: accommodation inside the campus, salary (3000 CNY/month for undergraduate and Master students and 3500 CNY/month for PhD students), and medical insurance.

Interested candidates can contact us via email (bambi-at-fudan.edu.cn).  


* The list includes universities in the following countries: Australia, Belgium, Canada, France, Germany, Ireland, Italy, Netherlands, New Zealand, Poland, Spain, Sweden, UK, USA.
