Modeling black hole X-ray emission

8-10 June 2022, online meeting

The purpose of this online mini-workshop is to review recent developments of public and non-public models for the emission of accreting black holes in the X-ray band.


Every talk is 30 minutes + 5 minutes for Q&A and we will have dedicated time for further discussion.
