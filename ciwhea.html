China-India Workshops

on High Energy Astrophysics

This is a workshop series to promote collaborations and mobility between the high energy astrophysics communities in China and India.


The first meeting was organized in 2020 (unfortunately online because of the pandemic and the associated travel restrictions) and we are committed to organizing a meeting every year.
