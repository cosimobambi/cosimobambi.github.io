China-India-Thailand Workshops

on High Energy Astrophysics

This is a workshop series to promote collaborations and mobility between the high energy astrophysics communities in China, India, and Thailand.


The first meeting was organized in 2020 as a China-India workshop (unfortunately online because of the pandemic and the associated travel restrictions) and we are committed to organizing a meeting every year.
